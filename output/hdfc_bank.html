Business descriptionGeneric
HDFC Bank is a leading Indian private sector bank offering a comprehensive suite of financial products and services to retail, wholesale, and commercial customers. It generates revenue primarily through net interest income from its extensive lending activities and fee-based income from various banking and financial services. The bank differentiates itself through a strong focus on digital innovation, a vast distribution network, and a diversified loan portfolio, including a significant housing finance franchise post-merger with HDFC Ltd.
