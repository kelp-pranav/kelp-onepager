Product-wise revenue mix (controlled-substance vs. non-controlled)
Embio Limited derives approximately 55-60% of its operating revenue from controlled substances, specifically ephedrine, pseudoephedrine, and norephedrine salts, as of fiscal year 2025.
High-margin vs. commodity split
While a precise percentage split between high-margin and commodity products is not explicitly stated, Embio focuses on manufacturing "complex APIs and intermediates" and "specialised products." The addition of Lisdexamfetamine Dimesylate, identified as a "high-margin product," significantly improved Embio's profitability in FY25, with the PBILDT margin rising to 25.35% (from 10.43% in the prior year) and profit after tax (PAT) margin increasing to 14.31% (from 2.47%).
Backward integration status for key APIs
Embio Limited is described as "one of the largest, totally integrated manufacturer in the world of Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients, Ephedrine, Pseudoephedrine and advanced intermediates." The company emphasizes "fully backward-integrated platforms" and its focus on backward-integrated operations is expected to support profit margins in the medium term. Embio plans to undertake a significant expansion project to enhance capabilities in API and intermediate production and support backward integration for Lisdexamfetamine Dimesylate.
