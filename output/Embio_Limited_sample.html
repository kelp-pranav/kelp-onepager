Business descriptionGeneric
Embio Limited, founded 1986 in Mumbai, manufactures controlled-substance APIs, chiral intermediates and specialty chemicals via fermentation and chiral chemistry at USFDA/WHO-GMP facilities, exporting to ~80 countries. It leads in licensed Schedule-1/2 controlled-substance APIs.
Investment thesisGeneric
Competitive moat. Leadership in licensed Schedule-1/2 controlled-substance APIs at USFDA/WHO-GMP facilities — a high regulatory barrier.
Current story. FY25 revenue +50.5% to ₹272.85 Cr with EBITDA margin expanding to 25.35%; CARE upgraded to BBB+/Stable.
Forward opportunity. Dahej capacity expansion plus True North growth capital position the company for export-led scale-up.
